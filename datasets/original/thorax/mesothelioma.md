# Mesothelioma in the Pleura, Pericardium and Peritoneum

**Quelle:** https://www.iccr-cancer.org/datasets/published-datasets/thorax/mesothelioma/

Datasets
>
Published Datasets
>
Thorax
>
Mesothelioma in the Pleura, Pericardium and Peritoneum
Scope
This dataset has been developed for biopsy and resection specimens of mesothelioma in the pleura, pericardium and peritoneum.

Publication History
Minor update to the 3rd edition – Version 3.1 September 2022
3rd edition
– September 2022
Updated following the publication of the 2021 World Health Organization (WHO) Classification of Thoracic Tumours, 5th edition.
To reference this dataset please use the following citation
: Klebe S, Brcic L, Galateau-Salle F, Dacic S, Roggli V, Nicholson A, Nowak A, Cooper W (2022).
Mesothelioma in the Pleura, Pericardium and Peritoneum Histopathology Reporting Guide. 3rd edition
. International Collaboration on Cancer Reporting; Sydney, Australia. ISBN: 978-1-922324-33-7.
Read more on the Mesothelioma in the Pleura and Peritoneum dataset (3nd edition) from the journal article written by the dataset authors:
Mesothelioma in the pleura, pericardium and peritoneum: Recommendations from the International Collaboration on Cancer Reporting (ICCR
). Klebe S, Judge M, Brcic L, Dacic S, Galateau-Salle F, Nicholson AG, Roggli V, Nowak AK, Cooper WA. Histopathology. 2024 Mar;84(4):633-645. doi: 10.1111/his.15106. Epub 2023 Dec 4.
2nd edition
– August 2017
Updated following the publication of the 8th edition of TNM staging.
To reference this dataset please use the following citation
