Endometriumkrebs
Umfang
Der Datensatz wurde für die pathologische Berichterstattung von Resektionsproben von Endometriumkarzinomen, einschließlich Karzinosarkomen, entwickelt. Es gilt nicht für kleine Endometriumbiopsieproben. Hämatopoetische Neoplasien, mesenchymale Neoplasien, Adenosarkome, maligne Melanome, andere nicht-epitheliale Malignome und metastatische Tumoren sind von diesem Datensatz ausgeschlossen. Adenosarkome und andere mesenchymale Neoplasien sind im ICCR-Datensatz zu bösartigen und potenziell bösartigen mesenchymalen Tumoren der Gebärmutter enthalten.

Publication History
Minor update to the 5th edition – Version 5.1 August 2024. Correction to FIGO Staging (2023 edition): II – Invasion of cervical stroma ‘with’ corrected to ‘without’.
5th edition
– August 2024
Incorporates the International Federation of Gynaecology and Obstetrics (FIGO) staging for endometrial cancer (2023) and includes changes to align the dataset with the World Health Organization (WHO) Classification of Tumours, Female Genital Tumours, 5th edition Corrigenda, July 2024.
This dataset was developed with the kind support of the International Society of Gynecological Pathologists (ISGyP).
SUMMARY OF CHANGES
• Revised guide to harmonise with ICCR style for value lists.
• Revised guide table for ‘Lymph node status’ element.
• Updated ‘Pathological staging’ guide element to include FIGO (2023 edition).
• Revised scope for clarity and to harmonise with ICCR style.
• Revised notes to harmonise with ICCR style, streamlined note length and references.
• Updated notes to reflect updated FIGO 2023 staging in elements: ‘Histological tumour type’, ‘Histological tumour grade’, ‘Myometrial invasion’, ‘Lymphovascular invasion’, ‘Cervical stroma’, ‘Parametria’, ‘Vagina’, ‘Uterine serosa’, ‘Adnexa’, ‘Lymph node status’, ‘Ancillary studies’, and ‘Provisional pathological staging’.
• Updated reference to the WHO Classification of Tumours, Female Genital Tumours, 5th edition Corrigenda, July 2024.
